Megisti

MEGISTI

It has been 100 years since founder, Nicholas Paspaley Snr, arrived in Australia.

Megisti is reflective of the momentous journey from Paspaley’s origin on the Greek island of Kastellorizo to the Kimberley.

Capturing the spirit of a journey between these two worlds, this high jewellery collection takes inspiration from unique rock formations and the intense blue waters that surround them.

Showcasing exceptional craftsmanship and innovation by our artisan designers and jewellers, this stunning and colourful collection is as versatile as it is timeless.
